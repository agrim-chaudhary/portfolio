About Me

Passionate Learner

I’m a CSE student at Delhi Technological University, originally from Ghaziabad, with a deep interest in building things that look good and work even better. I’ve always been drawn to the creative side of technology, and coding quickly became the place where logic and design finally met for me. Front-end development felt natural — the instant feedback, the ability to create interfaces from scratch, and the freedom to bring ideas to life through clean, responsive UI.

Alongside coding, video editing became my second creative outlet. I’ve always enjoyed the process of shaping moments into stories, whether it’s event edits, fast-paced reels, or aesthetic visual cuts. Editing taught me timing, storytelling, and how visuals can influence emotion — skills that strangely blend well with front-end development, where design and flow matter just as much as functionality.
